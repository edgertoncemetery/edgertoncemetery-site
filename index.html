About the Cemetery

Edgerton Cemetery is a private, multi-family cemetery cared for by family members across generations.

Location

3061 Nahunta Rd, Pikeville, NC
(beside the Nahunta Fire Department)

Current Care (as of 2026)

Sonny Edgerton (Nahunta)
with help from Jennifer Skinner (Kenly)

Established

Deed dated March 21, 1938
Trustees: Cassie Edgerton Lynch, Floyd Edgerton, Girney Edgerton

The cemetery contains approximately 150 burials. We also know there are others buried here who were never documented.

Purpose

The Edgerton Cemetery Maintenance Trust exists to ensure long-term care, mowing, and preservation so upkeep does not fall on any one individual.
