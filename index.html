Edgerton Cemetery

Edgerton Cemetery is a private, multi-family cemetery located at 3061 Nahunta Road, beside the Nahunta Fire Department, North Carolina.

As of 2026, the cemetery is being looked after primarily by Sonny Edgerton (Nahunta) and Jennifer Skinner (Kenly).

The cemetery was formally established through a deed dated March 21, 1938, in which W. F. Flowers (widower) conveyed land to Cassie E. Lynch, Floyd Edgerton, and Girney Edgerton, who were named as Trustees of the Edgerton Burial Ground. This reflects the original intent that the land be held in trust for burial purposes and cared for on behalf of the families connected to this burial ground.

Established around 1940, the cemetery contains approximately 150 burials and has been cared for by family members across generations.

Purpose

The Edgerton Cemetery Maintenance Trust was established to help ensure the long-term care, mowing, and preservation of the cemetery so that upkeep does not fall on any one individual.
