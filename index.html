Edgerton Cemetery

Edgerton Cemetery is a private, multi family cemetery located at 3061 Nahunta Road, beside the Nahunta Fire Department, North Carolina. Established around 1940, the cemetery contains approximately 150 family burials and has been cared for by family members for generations. A deed was made on the 21st of March, 1938 by and between W.F. Flowers (widower) and Cassie E. Lynch, Floyd Edgerton and Girney Edgerton, Trustees of the Edgerton Burial Ground.

Purpose

The Edgerton Cemetery Maintenance Trust was established to help ensure the long-term care, mowing, and preservation of the cemetery so that upkeep does not fall on any one individual.

Contributions are voluntary and are used solely for routine maintenance and preservation of the cemetery.
